5. Age Criteria
The age limit for the children to be admitted at the entry level classes as on 31-03-2024 is as under:

  • For LKG/Pre-School: Between 3-4 years
  • For UKG/Pre-Primary: Between 4-5 years
  • For Class I: Between 5-6 years

6. Documents to be Submitted at the Time of Registration (Offline)

  • Copy of Birth Certificate of the child issued by the MCD/appropriate authority duly attested by parent of child.
  • Passport-size photo of child, mother, and father.
  • Self-attested copy of one of the following documents as proof of residence:
    • Ration card issued in the name of parents (mother/father bearing the name of the child).
    • Domicile certificate of child or parents.
    • Voter I-card (EPIC) of any of the parents.
    • Electricity bill/MTNL telephone bill/Water bill/Passport in the name of any of the parents or child.
    • Aadhar Card/UID card issued in the name of any of the parents.
  • Siblings: Copy of School Identity Card/Report Card/Fee Slip (if applicable).
  • In case the child is the first born, an undertaking stating the same must be given by the parents.
  • Alumni: Passing Certificate from Vishal Bharti Sr. Sec. School (if applicable).
  • Attested copy of Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC) issued by Revenue Department, if any.
Note:

1. Information filled in admission form must strictly match / should be in sync with the supporting documents.

2. All documents in original must be brought for verification at the time of admission.

Admission Procedure for Classes 2nd Onwards
1. Admission to new students for classes 2nd onwards is made in February of the preceding session up to 31st August, depending on the availability of seats.
2. Admissions are subject to entrance test, interview/oral test of the child, and interview with the parents.
3. No admissions will be made after 31st August in the current year except for children of transferable parents or in special cases.

Documents to be submitted at the time of admission

  • Admission form duly filled.
  • School Leaving Certificate from a government-recognized school duly attested by the education office.

Admission and Withdrawal Rules

Admission Rules

Registration for admission in Class Pre-School, Pre-Primary, and Class 1st is open in the month of November/December, strictly as per the guidelines issued by the Directorate of Education.

Admission in classes from 2nd onwards is made from the month of February of the preceding academic session till 31st August. No admission is made after 31st August in the current year.

Withdrawal Rules

  1. A clear one month’s notice or one month’s fee in lieu thereof is necessary if a parent wants to withdraw their child from the school after the beginning of the session.
  2. Transfer Certificate/School Leaving Certificate will not be issued unless all dues are settled.

Procedure for Withdrawal

1. Contact the school reception with a written request application for withdrawal.

2. Obtain the School Leaving Form and fill it with correct information.

3. The School Leaving Certificate will not be issued unless all dues are settled.

Name Struck Off (NSO)

The name of the student will be struck off the rolls under the following circumstances:

  • Continued absence from school without a leave application.
  • Indiscipline behavior towards school management, principal, staff members, and other students.
  • Non-payment of fees and dues.
